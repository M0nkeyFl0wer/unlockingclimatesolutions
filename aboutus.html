About US

What is The Elephant Room?

Climate Change is the Elephant in the Room

Stories about the climate crisis need to be around every corner and yet, they are oddly absent. While the majority of people understand that climate change is real, there is still no feeling of urgency and rarely are people aware of the solutions available to usour mission is to change that.

We Organize And Implement Strategic Climate Campaigns With Businesses, Non-Profits, Governments Of All Sizes And Other Partners To Spread Awareness, Motivate Change And Make Solutions A Reality.

Public Engagement

Weve Been Involved In Some Of The Most Impactful Public Engagement Campaigns In The Past Decades Related To Climate Change.

Reaching Beyond 'The Choir'

 We Find Creative Ways To Reach People In Their Own Communities And Talk To Them About How The Climate Crisis Affects Them And What They Can Do About It. 

Finding New Heroes

We Find, Support And Amplify The Voices Of Trusted Community Members In A Wide Variety Of Stakeholder Groups And Help Them Bring New People Into Working On Climate Solutions Together.


Making It Personal

We Are Dedicated To Communicating The Urgency Of The Climate Crisis To People In A Way That Is  Relevant To Them And Motivates Them To Act.

Supporting Solutions

We Encourage And Actively Support Groups And Individuals Working To Solve The Climate Crisis Whether They Are Using Conventional Or Innovative Means To Reach Their Goals. 

Remote Co-Learning Events

A series of multi-disciplinary forums for different climate change solutions initiatives:


Supply Chain Optimization, Monitoring and Reporting Verification, Carbon Accounting, Renewable Energy Financing and Local Energy Grids, and more.
